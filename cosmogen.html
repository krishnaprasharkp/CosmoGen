About Us

CosmoGen Students Society is the only STEM-based society at GNDU, founded on 20th September 2023 by Krishna Prashar. With a vision to uplift the coding culture, CosmoGen serves as a bridge between students and MNCs, providing them with real-world opportunities, technical expertise, and industry exposure.

Message from the Founder

Mr. Krishna Prashar

Welcome to CosmoGen! Our mission is to create a thriving tech community at GNDU, where students are equipped with knowledge, skills, and opportunities to excel in the industry. We believe in innovation, collaboration, and the power of learning together. Join us in shaping the future of technology!
